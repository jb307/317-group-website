Parking Tips for Visitors

Parking Tips:
- Do not leave anything that might seem valuable exposed and unattended in car (even empty backpacks are enough for someone to break in).
- If possible take valuable items with you, try not to leave any incentive for thieves.
- Popular spots for tourists are also popular spots for car break ins so be weary when parking in places like pier 39 or the Haight.
- NEVER keep a spare key in or on your vehicle.
- Try to park in well lit areas with lots of foot traffic.
